Australia - Financial Overview

Australia, as a developed country, has a strong financial market and stable economy. Here’s an overview of its financial aspects.

Australia

GDP: $1.5 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 30%.

Population: Approximately 26 million.

Financial Market: Australia’s financial market includes the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) and is known for its stability and innovation.
