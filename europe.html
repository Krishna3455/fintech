Europe - Financial Overview

Europe boasts a mix of advanced economies and emerging markets. Here’s an overview of financial aspects across key European countries.

Germany

GDP: $4.5 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 30%.

Population: Approximately 83 million.

Financial Market: Germany’s financial market includes the Frankfurt Stock Exchange (FWB) and is a major player in Europe.

France

GDP: $3.1 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 25%.

Population: Approximately 67 million.

Financial Market: France’s financial market includes the Euronext Paris and has a robust investment environment.

United Kingdom

GDP: $3.1 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 19%.

Population: Approximately 68 million.

Financial Market: The UK’s financial market includes the London Stock Exchange (LSE) and is a key global financial hub.

Italy

GDP: $2.0 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 24%.

Population: Approximately 60 million.

Financial Market: Italy’s financial market includes the Milan Stock Exchange (Borsa Italiana) with a focus on diverse investments.
