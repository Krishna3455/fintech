North America - Financial Overview

North America features a mix of advanced and emerging economies. Here’s a snapshot of the financial landscape in key countries of this region.

United States

GDP: $25.3 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 21%.

Population: Approximately 332 million.

Financial Market: The US has a highly developed financial market with major stock exchanges including the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) and NASDAQ.

Canada

GDP: $2.1 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 15%.

Population: Approximately 38 million.

Financial Market: Canada’s financial market is well-established with the Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX) being a major player.

Mexico

GDP: $1.4 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 30%.

Population: Approximately 126 million.

Financial Market: Mexico’s financial market includes the Mexican Stock Exchange (BMV) and is growing with increasing foreign investments.
