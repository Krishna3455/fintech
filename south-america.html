South America - Financial Overview

South America is home to various emerging markets with significant growth potential. Here’s an overview of key financial aspects across major South American countries.

Brazil

GDP: $1.9 trillion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 15% to 25%.

Population: Approximately 213 million.

Financial Market: Brazil’s financial market includes the B3 Exchange and is a major player in Latin America.

Argentina

GDP: $640 billion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 30%.

Population: Approximately 45 million.

Financial Market: Argentina’s financial market includes the Buenos Aires Stock Exchange (BYMA) with a focus on investment opportunities.

Chile

GDP: $490 billion (2022)

Tax System: Progressive income tax system with corporate tax rates around 27%.

Population: Approximately 19 million.

Financial Market: Chile’s financial market includes the Santiago Stock Exchange (BCS) with a stable investment environment.
